About Me

I am a former Structural Engineer turned Data Scientist. My skills combine techniques across the science and engineering scopes of what is commonly referred to as Data Science today.

Passion and Hard Work form the core of my approach. I solve problems by asking the right questions. Persisting data and insights derived from the data is important in this field.

  • Technologies

Technologies

Tech I've had significant experience with, not just a ten minute tutorial in, include:
Python, R, Plotly, Dash, Docker, and Redis. I have built models for most supervised learning algorithms. Additionally, I have experience building and deploying apps with NLP based backends, Word Embeddings and Rule Based models.
